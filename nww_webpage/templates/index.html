About NORML Women of Washington

The National Organization for the Reform of Marijuana Laws (NORML) is the United States' oldest marijuana consumer’s lobby. The NORML Women of Washington is a women’s group dedicated to education and outreach on marijuana in the State of Washington. We also promote the NORMLization of marijuana by challenging the myths and stereotypes associated with its use, users, and supporters. The US has decades of propaganda, misinformation, and harsh criminal enforcement to overcome. We will fight to ensure that justice stays at the forefront as this movement becomes an industry. We are a collaborative group with many diverse backgrounds and interests. We welcome you to join us.
